Privacy Policy for Mood Check Log

Last updated: May 29, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Mood Check Log handles user data. Mood Check Log is a simple personal check-in app for recording mood, energy, stress, tags, short notes, and date-based entries.

Mood Check Log is not a medical, therapy, diagnosis, or treatment app. It is designed only as a private personal logging tool.

1. Data You Enter in the App

Mood Check Log allows you to enter and save the following information:

  • Mood score
  • Energy score
  • Stress score
  • Selected tag, such as Work, Sleep, Food, Social, Health, or Other
  • Short optional notes
  • Date and time of each check-in

This information is stored locally on your device using local app storage. The app does not require an account, login, or server connection to save these check-ins.

2. Local Storage and Retention

Mood Check Log stores your check-in records on your device. These records remain on your device until you delete them, clear the app data, or uninstall the app.

Mood Check Log does not operate its own server for storing your mood, energy, stress, tag, or note entries.

3. How You Can Delete Your Data

You can delete your saved check-in records in the following ways:

  • Delete individual check-ins inside the app, if available.
  • Clear the app's data from your Android device settings.
  • Uninstall the app from your device.

Because Mood Check Log does not use accounts or a developer-operated server for check-in records, uninstalling the app or clearing app data removes the locally stored records from that device.

4. Advertising

Mood Check Log may display advertisements using Google AdMob or other Google advertising services. These advertising services may collect or process data such as:

  • Advertising ID
  • Device information
  • Approximate app interaction data
  • Ad performance and diagnostic information

This data may be used to provide, maintain, measure, and improve advertising services, including personalized or non-personalized ads depending on your settings and applicable laws.

You can learn more about how Google uses data from apps that use its services here: https://policies.google.com/technologies/partner-sites

5. Data Sharing

Mood Check Log does not sell your check-in records. The mood, energy, stress, tag, note, and date entries you create are stored locally on your device and are not sent to a developer-operated server.

Third-party advertising services may process advertising-related data as described in the Advertising section above.

6. App Permissions

Mood Check Log may require internet access to load and display advertisements. The app does not require an account to use its main check-in features.

7. Children

Mood Check Log is not directed to children under the age of 13. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe that a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact us.

8. Security

Mood Check Log stores check-in records locally on your device. You are responsible for maintaining the security of your device. No method of electronic storage is completely secure, but the app is designed to avoid sending your personal check-in records to a developer-operated server.

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date.
