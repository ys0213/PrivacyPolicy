1. Information We Collect

Our apps are designed to be simple local apps. We do not require users to create an account, log in, or submit personal information to our own server.

Some apps allow users to manually enter and save app data such as notes, logs, counters, scores, settings, records, or game progress. This data is stored locally on the user's device and is used only to provide the app's features.

Depending on the app and version, third-party advertising services may collect or process certain information for advertising, analytics, fraud prevention, and service improvement. This may include:

  • Advertising ID
  • Device information, such as device model, operating system, and app version
  • Approximate location, where applicable
  • IP address and network information
  • App usage events related to ad delivery and performance
  • Crash, diagnostics, and performance information

2. Purpose of Data Collection

Locally stored app data is used to provide app features, such as saving records, showing summaries, keeping settings, and preserving game or app progress.

Advertising-related data may be used by third-party advertising services to:

  • Display personalized or non-personalized advertisements
  • Measure ad performance
  • Prevent fraud and abuse
  • Improve advertising services
  • Analyze app stability and performance

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell users' personal information. We do not operate our own server to collect or store users' local app records.

Depending on the app and version, data may be collected or processed by third-party advertising and app service providers, including but not limited to Start.io, Appodeal, Google AdMob, and Google Play services.
