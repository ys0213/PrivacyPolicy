1. Information We Collect

These apps do not require account registration and do not directly collect personally identifiable information such as your name, email address, phone number, contacts, photos, or precise location.

The apps may store limited game-related data locally on your device, such as story progress, choices, unlocked endings, achievements, settings, and hint status. This data is stored only on your device and is used to provide the game experience.

Third-party advertising services used in these apps may collect or process limited data for advertising, analytics, fraud prevention, and service improvement purposes. This may include:

  • Advertising ID
  • Device information, such as device model, operating system version, and language settings
  • App usage data related to ad delivery and performance
  • Network-related information, such as IP address or approximate location derived from IP address

2. How Information Is Used

Information may be used for the following purposes:

  • To provide and maintain the game experience
  • To save local story progress, endings, achievements, and settings
  • To display personalized or non-personalized advertisements
  • To measure ad performance and prevent fraud or abuse
  • To improve app stability and performance

3. Third-Party Advertising Services

These apps may use third-party advertising services, including Start.io. Start.io may collect and process data according to its own privacy policy.

Users can manage advertising preferences through their device settings, including resetting or limiting use of the Advertising ID where supported by the device.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal information. Data may be shared with third-party advertising partners only as needed for ad delivery, measurement, fraud prevention, and related services.

5. Local Data Storage

Story progress, choices, endings, achievements, settings, and similar gameplay data may be stored locally on your device. This data is not sent to our own server. You may delete this data by using the reset option in the app or by clearing the app data from your device settings.

6. Children's Privacy

These apps are not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you believe that a child has provided personal information, please contact us so that appropriate action can be taken.

We use reasonable measures to keep the apps simple and secure. However, no method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.
