Privacy Policy for Caffeine Limit Log

Effective date: June 2, 2026

Caffeine Limit Log is a simple caffeine tracking app. This Privacy Policy explains how the app handles information related to caffeine entries, daily limits, local history, advertising, storage, retention, and deletion.

1. Information You Enter in the App

Caffeine Limit Log allows users to manually enter caffeine-related records. Depending on how you use the app, this may include:

  • Caffeine amount in milligrams
  • Drink or caffeine source type, such as coffee, tea, soda, energy drink, supplement, or other
  • Optional notes entered by the user
  • Selected or logged dates
  • Personal daily caffeine limit
  • Calendar-based caffeine history generated from your entries

2. Health and Wellness Information

Caffeine entries and daily caffeine limits may be considered health or wellness-related information because they relate to personal caffeine intake habits.

The app is designed only for simple personal tracking. It does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, treatment, clinical decision support, disease prevention guidance, or emergency assistance. Caffeine sensitivity can vary by person. If you have medical questions about caffeine intake, you should consult a qualified healthcare professional.

3. Local Storage

Caffeine Limit Log stores the caffeine entries, daily limit, notes, and calendar history locally on your device using app storage or local web storage. This information is used only to show your daily caffeine total, remaining amount, limit status, and calendar history inside the app.

The app does not require an account. The app does not upload your caffeine entries, notes, daily limit, or calendar history to a developer-operated server.

4. How Your Information Is Used

Information entered in the app is used to provide the app's core features, including:

  • Calculating your daily caffeine total
  • Comparing your caffeine total with your personal daily limit
  • Showing within-limit, near-limit, or over-limit status
  • Displaying caffeine history on the calendar
  • Allowing you to add, edit, and delete caffeine entries

5. Data Sharing

The developer does not sell your caffeine entries, notes, daily limit, or local calendar history. The developer does not share your caffeine entries, notes, daily limit, or local calendar history with third parties through a developer-operated server.

6. Advertising

Caffeine Limit Log may display advertisements using Google AdMob or the Google Mobile Ads SDK.

The Google Mobile Ads SDK may automatically collect and share certain data for advertising, analytics, and fraud prevention purposes. Depending on your device, settings, region, consent choices, SDK version, and Google services configuration, this may include information such as IP address, app interactions, diagnostic information, device or account identifiers, advertising ID, and related advertising data.

Google may use this information in accordance with its own policies and technologies. You can learn more from Google here: Google Advertising Policy and Google Mobile Ads SDK Data Disclosure .

7. Data Retention

Caffeine entries, notes, daily limits, and calendar history are stored locally on your device until you delete them, clear the app's storage, or uninstall the app.

Because the app does not use a developer-operated account system or developer-operated server storage for caffeine logs, the developer does not maintain a separate server-side copy of your caffeine entries.

8. How to Delete Your Data

You can delete your caffeine data in the following ways:

  • Delete individual caffeine entries inside the app.
  • Clear entries for a selected date or today, if the feature is available in your app version.
  • Clear the app's data from Android system settings.
  • Uninstall the app from your device.

Deleting app data or uninstalling the app removes locally stored caffeine entries, notes, daily limits, and calendar history from your device. Data processed by Google AdMob or Google services is controlled by Google's own policies and user controls.

9. Permissions

Caffeine Limit Log does not require access to sensitive device permissions such as location, contacts, camera, microphone, SMS, phone calls, or body sensor data for its core caffeine logging features.

If advertisements are enabled, the app may use internet and network access to load ads. If the app includes the Android advertising ID permission, it is used for advertising purposes by the advertising SDK, subject to user settings and applicable Google policies.

10. Children

Caffeine Limit Log is not directed to children. The app is intended for general personal tracking by users who are allowed to use the app under the laws and platform rules that apply in their region.

11. Security

Locally stored app data remains on your device unless you delete it, clear app data, or uninstall the app. No method of electronic storage or transmission is completely secure, but the app is designed to avoid storing caffeine entries on a developer-operated server.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. If the policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new effective date.
