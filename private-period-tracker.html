Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy explains how this application handles user data.

1. Data Stored on Device

This app may allow users to enter and save app-related records, settings, preferences, and reminder settings. For Private Period Tracker, this may include period records, cycle settings, period length settings, prediction preferences, and reminder settings.

User-entered tracking data is stored locally on the user's device. The app does not require an account and does not upload user-entered tracking data to our servers.

2. Data Collected for Advertising

This app may collect limited data through Google AdMob for advertising purposes, such as Advertising ID, device information, app interaction data, diagnostics, and approximate usage data.

3. Purpose of Data Use

  • To provide app features such as local records, settings, predictions, and reminders
  • To store user-entered data locally on the device
  • To display personalized or non-personalized advertisements
  • To analyze app performance and improve stability

4. Data Sharing

User-entered records and settings stored locally on the device are not shared with us and are not stored on our servers.

Advertising-related data may be collected and processed by advertising networks such as Google AdMob according to their own privacy practices.

5. Data Retention

User-entered records, settings, preferences, and reminder settings are stored locally on the user's device and are retained until the user deletes them in the app, clears the app data through Android system settings, or uninstalls the app.

Because the app does not store user-entered records on our servers, we do not retain this user-entered data outside the user's device.

6. Data Deletion

Users can delete saved data at any time within the app if the app provides a delete or reset option. In Private Period Tracker, users can delete saved period records and prediction settings by opening Edit and selecting Delete all data.

Users can also delete locally stored app data through Android system settings or by uninstalling the app.

Because the app does not require an account and does not store user-entered records on our servers, there is no server-side account data to delete.

7. Third-Party Services

This application may use Google AdMob. Google may collect and process data according to Google's own privacy policy and advertising policies.

8. Children's Privacy

This application is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Medical and Health Disclaimer

For apps that provide health, cycle, or wellness tracking features, the information shown is for general tracking and informational purposes only. It does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, treatment, or contraception guidance.
