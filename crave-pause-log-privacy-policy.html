Privacy Policy for Crave Pause Log

Effective date: June 2, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how Crave Pause Log handles information when you use the app. Crave Pause Log is a personal snack craving and behavior logging app. It allows users to record snack cravings, craving intensity, triggers, alternative actions, outcomes, optional notes, and calendar-based logs.

Crave Pause Log does not require an account and does not use a developer-operated server or database to store your craving logs.

1. Information You Enter in the App

Crave Pause Log allows you to enter and save the following types of information:

  • Snack craving date and time
  • Craving intensity
  • Craving trigger, such as stress, boredom, late night, after meal, work break, seeing food, or other
  • Alternative action, such as drinking water, waiting, walking, brushing teeth, tea or coffee, planned snack, or no action
  • Outcome, such as paused craving, used alternative, planned snack, unplanned snack, or still craving
  • Optional notes entered by the user

These records may relate to food behavior, habits, wellness, or nutrition-related self-reflection. The app does not ask for your name, phone number, account login, precise location, payment information, or medical records.

2. How Your App Data Is Stored

The snack craving logs and settings you enter in Crave Pause Log are stored locally on your device using the app's local storage. They are not sent to a developer-operated server.

Because the records are stored locally, they are generally available only on the device and app installation where they were created, unless you manually copy or export them using a backup feature provided in the app.

3. How Your Information Is Used

The information you enter is used only to provide the app's core features, including:

  • Saving and displaying your snack craving logs
  • Showing records by date in the calendar view
  • Calculating simple summaries, such as today's log count, 7-day success rate, average intensity, and top trigger
  • Allowing you to review, edit, delete, or copy a backup of your saved logs

Crave Pause Log does not use your entered craving logs for medical diagnosis, treatment, professional nutrition advice, or guaranteed weight loss results.

4. Data Sharing

The developer of Crave Pause Log does not sell your personal data and does not share your locally saved craving logs with third parties.

If you choose to copy, export, or share backup data outside the app, that action is controlled by you and may be handled by the other apps or services you choose to use.

5. Advertising

Crave Pause Log may display advertisements through Google AdMob. AdMob and related Google services may collect or process certain information, such as advertising identifiers, device information, approximate location derived from network information, app interactions, diagnostics, and other data, depending on your device settings, consent choices, region, and Google services.

This advertising data is handled by Google according to Google's policies and is not used by the developer to identify you personally.

6. Data Retention

Your snack craving logs remain stored on your device until you delete them, clear the app's data, or uninstall the app.

The developer does not maintain a separate server-side copy of your craving logs.

7. How to Delete Your Data

You can delete your data in the following ways:

  • Delete individual craving logs using the delete option in the app.
  • Clear all saved app data using the app's data management option, if available.
  • Clear the app's storage from your Android device settings.
  • Uninstall the app from your device.

Because Crave Pause Log does not require an account and does not store your craving logs on a developer-operated server, there is no separate account deletion process for this app.

8. Backup and Export

The app may provide an option to copy a backup of your saved logs. If you use this feature, the copied data may be placed on your device clipboard or shared through tools you choose. You are responsible for where you store or send exported backup data.

9. Health and Wellness Disclaimer

Crave Pause Log is a personal tracking and self-reflection tool. It is not a medical device and does not provide medical advice, diagnosis, treatment, professional nutrition advice, or emergency services.

If you have concerns about your health, eating behavior, nutrition, mental health, or any medical condition, please consult a qualified healthcare professional.

10. Children's Privacy

Crave Pause Log is not directed to children under the age of 13. The app does not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe that a child has provided personal information through the app, please contact the developer so appropriate action can be taken.

11. Security

The app stores user-entered logs locally on the user's device. No method of electronic storage is completely secure, but the app is designed to avoid sending your craving logs to a developer-operated server.

You are responsible for protecting access to your device, including using device lock features where appropriate.

12. Changes to This Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect changes in the app, legal requirements, advertising SDKs, or data handling practices. Any updates will be posted on this page with a revised effective date.
